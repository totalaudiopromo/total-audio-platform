## Handling Questions and Interruptions

### üéØ If Questions Interrupt Flow

**Tactics:**

| Question Type | Response Strategy |
|---------------|-------------------|
| **Tactical Questions (Sam)** | Answer immediately (shows engagement): "Great question - yes, it exports to CSV for MailChimp integration. Let me show you that after this section." |
| **Budget Questions (Dan)** | Note for ROI section: "Perfect timing - I'm covering pricing in 2 minutes. Short answer: ¬£19/month per user saves 20 hours/week." |
| **Strategic Questions (Bee/Adam)** | Note for Q&A: "That's a great strategic question about scaling - let's discuss in detail after the demo so I can give it proper attention." |
| **Technical Questions** | Answer briefly, offer follow-up: "It's built on Next.js with AI pipeline integration. Happy to do a technical deep-dive after we cover the business value." |

**Acknowledge and Defer Script:**
"Great question, Dan. Let me finish showing you this section, then I'll answer that specifically - I want to make sure you see the full picture first."

**Answer Quickly and Resume:**
"Yes, absolutely. [Give 30-second answer]. Let me show you that in action right now..."

**Incorporate into Demo:**
"Perfect timing - I'm about to show you exactly that in the next section."

### üö® If They're Not Engaged (Warning Signs)

**Warning Signs:**
- Checking phones frequently
- Side conversations
- Crossed arms, leaning back
- No eye contact
- No questions at all

**Recovery Tactics:**

| Tactic | When to Use | Script |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| **Call Them Directly** | After 2 min of disengagement | "Sam, does this match your workflow? Am I missing something important?" |
| **Speed Up** | If they look bored | "Let me speed through this - the key point is [value prop]. Let me show you ROI instead." |
| **Ask Direct Question** | If unclear what's wrong | "I'm sensing some hesitation - what concerns do you have? Should I slow down, speed up, or focus on something specific?" |
| **Check Temperature** | If completely lost | "Before I continue, what questions do you have? I want to make sure this is valuable for you." |
| **Pivot to ROI Early** | If demo isn't landing | "Let me skip to ROI - here's what this means for Liberty's bottom line..." [Jump to ROI slides] |

**Nuclear Option:**
If demo is completely failing and they're disengaged:
"You know what, let me stop the demo and just have a conversation. What's the biggest pain point in your workflow right now? Let me show you exactly how we'd solve that."
