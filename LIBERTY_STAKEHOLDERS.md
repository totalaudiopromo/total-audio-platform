# Liberty Music PR - Stakeholder Intelligence

Four key decision-makers will influence Total Audio Promo's adoption at Liberty Music PR. Each has distinct priorities, backgrounds, and concerns that require tailored messaging:

1. **Bee Adamic (CEO, Founder)** - Vision & Mission Alignment
2. **Adam Al-Hilali (Co-Founder)** - Innovation & Digital Strategy

**Pitch Strategy:** Build enthusiasm with Sam (daily user), demonstrate ROI to Dan (operations), align with vision for Bee (mission), and position as innovation for Adam (digital mindset).
