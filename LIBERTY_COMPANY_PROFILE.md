# Liberty Music PR - Company Profile

## Executive Summary

Liberty Music PR is an independent digital music PR agency founded in 2016 by Bee Adamic and Adam Al-Hilali. Starting from their Brighton flat, the company has grown to 40+ freelancers working with artists worldwide, with offices in Brighton, London, and LA, and plans for a New York office. They specialise in empowering independent and emerging artists through comprehensive digital PR services.

**Founded:** May 2016
**Founders:** Bee Adamic (CEO) and Adam Al-Hilali (Founder, Digital Entrepreneur)
**Team Size:** 40+ freelancers
**Locations:** Brighton (HQ), London, LA (with NY office planned)
**Focus:** Independent and alternative music, emerging artists
**Recognition:** Listed among UK's Top 10 music PR companies in 2025
