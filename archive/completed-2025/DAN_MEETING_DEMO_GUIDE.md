**Q: "Can we customize the branding completely?"**
A: Yes - agency name, logo (upload your own), primary/secondary colors, contact email. All appear on PDF exports. No "Powered by Audio Intel" watermark on Agency plan.

**Q: "What if we have 10 team members?"**
A: Unlimited team members on Agency plan. Role-based permissions (Admin/Member/Client) for granular control.

**Q: "How does this work with existing workflows?"**
A: Import contacts via CSV, create campaigns with familiar Excel-style interface, export white-label PDFs for clients. Minimal learning curve.

**Q: "What about data security and client confidentiality?"**
A: Row-level security in database, team member activity logging, role-based permissions. Enterprise-grade Supabase infrastructure.

**Q: "Can clients log in to see their campaigns?"**
A: Yes - invite them as "Client" role (read-only). They see campaign progress but can't edit. Perfect for client transparency.

**Q: "What's the difference between Professional and Agency plans?"**

- **Professional (¬£19/month)**: Individual use, basic features, "Powered by" watermark
