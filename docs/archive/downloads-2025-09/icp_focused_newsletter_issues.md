## Issue #1: Three Features Built by Weekend Warriors

_Week of 1st September 2025_

Right, so this week I learned that most Audio Intel users do their campaign planning on Sundays...

Turns out indie artists aren't researching contacts during business hours - they're doing it when they have time. Which is usually Sunday afternoon whilst everyone else is watching football.

---

## What Got Built This Week

### Feature #1: Batch Upload (Because One Campaign = Multiple Files)

**The Reality:**Got a message from a Leeds electronic artist: "I've got separate spreadsheets for radio, blogs, and playlists. Can I upload them all at once instead of switching between tabs?"

**Why This Matters:**Solo artists organize contacts differently than agencies. They might have "Radio 1 contacts," "Local blog list," and "Spotify curators" as separate files.

**Built in 45 minutes:**Upload multiple CSV files simultaneously, see progress for each campaign type.

**User Response:**"Just uploaded radio, blog, and playlist contacts whilst making lunch. Saved an hour of switching between uploads."

### Feature #2: "Will This Email Bounce?" Preview

**The Problem:**Manchester indie band: "Spent Saturday researching 30 contacts, then 8 emails bounced. Made me look proper amateur."

**The Fix:**Show email validation results before you commit. No more embarrassing bounced emails killing your credibility.

**Real Example:**Preview showed 5 out of 23 contacts had dead emails. Artist fixed them before sending, got 3 responses instead of looking unprofessional.

### Feature #3: "How Long Will This Take?" Timer

**User Frustration:**"Started contact research at 9pm thinking it would take 10 minutes. Still going at 11pm."

**Solution:**Shows exactly how long processing will take based on your tier and file size.

**Why It Helps:**Pro users see "60 seconds" - worth the upgrade. Free users see "2-3 minutes" - time for a cup of tea, not a full evening lost.

**Original Plan:**Confidence percentages for contact accuracy
**User Feedback:**"I don't care if you're 94% confident about someone who hasn't posted in 6 months"
