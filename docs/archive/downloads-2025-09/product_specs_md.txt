## üîç Audio Intel - Contact Intelligence & Enrichment

### Current Status: LIVE at intel.totalaudiopromo.com

#### Core Functionality
**Purpose**: Transform basic email addresses into comprehensive industry contact profiles with submission preferences, recent activity, and optimal outreach timing.

#### User Workflow
1. **Input**: User uploads CSV/enters email addresses
