## Response Guidelines

### DO:
- **Acknowledge their success** or point first
- **Position Audio Intel** as complementary or foundational
- **Use specific statistics** (94%, 15+ hours, 2 minutes, £19.99)
- **Stay professional** and supportive
- **Include clear value proposition**

### DON'T:
- **Directly criticise** competitors
- **Be defensive** or argumentative
- **Hijack their thread** with lengthy explanations
- **Copy their exact language** or approach
- **Sound sales-y** or pushy

### Timing:
- **Wait 2-4 hours** after their post for natural engagement
- **Don't be the first** to comment/reply
- **Add value** to the conversation, don't just promote
- **Engage genuinely** with others in the thread first

## Content Inspiration from Competitor Posts

### Turn Their Success Into Your Content
- **"While others celebrate submission success..."** → Create post about contact ownership
- **"While others charge per pitch..."** → Create cost comparison content
- **"While others act as gatekeepers..."** → Create direct access content
- **"While others focus on US markets..."** → Create UK-focused content

### Use Their Topics, Own the Angle
- If they post about **playlist success** → You post about **finding playlist contacts**
- If they post about **submission tips** → You post about **research efficiency**
- If they post about **industry insights** → You post about **contact intelligence**
- If they post about **artist wins** → You post about **time savings wins**
