## Keyword Validation Process

### Step 1: Search Volume Validation
Use Google Keyword Planner to verify:
- **Minimum 50 monthly searches** for long-tail
- **Minimum 200 monthly searches** for primary keywords
- **Trending upward** or stable over 12 months

### Step 2: Competition Analysis
Check top 10 competitors for each keyword:
- **Domain Authority** of ranking sites
- **Content quality** of ranking pages  
- **Paid ad competition** (high = good commercial intent)

### Step 3: Intent Alignment
Map keywords to user journey:
- **Problem Aware**: "music promotion challenges"
- **Solution Aware**: "contact enrichment tools"  
- **Product Aware**: "audio intel alternative"
- **Purchase Ready**: "audio intel pricing"

## Validation Metrics & KPIs

### Keyword Performance Targets
- **Organic CTR**: Above 3% for target keywords
- **Conversion Rate**: Above 5% for commercial keywords  
- **Cost Per Conversion**: Under £30 for paid search

### Monthly Reporting
Track progress on:
- **Keyword rankings** (target top 10 for 20+ keywords)
- **Organic traffic growth** (target 25% month-over-month)

- **Content engagement** (time on page, bounce rate)
