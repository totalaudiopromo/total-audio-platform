# Audio Intel Facebook Ads + AI Avatar Strategy

## Video Content Strategy (Using HeyGen + ElevenLabs)

### Avatar Persona: "Music Industry Expert"
- **Character**: Professional music industry consultant
- **Voice**: ElevenLabs professional male/female voice
- **Setting**: Modern music studio background
- **Wardrobe**: Smart casual (music industry appropriate)

### Video Scripts (30-60 seconds each)

#### Video 1: Problem Identification
**Hook**: "Spending 15 hours a week researching playlist curators?"
**Body**: Show painful manual research vs Audio Intel automation
**CTA**: "Get your free contact sample at intel.totalaudiopromo.com"

#### Video 2: Results/Social Proof
**Hook**: "This music PR agency enriched 2,000 contacts in 5 minutes"
**Body**: Show before/after spreadsheet transformation
**CTA**: "Try Audio Intel free - link in bio"

#### Video 3: Behind-the-Scenes
**Hook**: "15 years in music PR taught me this one thing..."
**Body**: Founder story + industry credibility
**CTA**: "Book a free demo to see how it works"

### HeyGen Production Workflow
1. **Script Input**: Upload final scripts
2. **Avatar Selection**: Professional presenter style
3. **Voice Cloning**: Use ElevenLabs for consistent brand voice
4. **Background**: Music studio/office setting
5. **Export**: 1080p MP4 for Facebook optimal quality
