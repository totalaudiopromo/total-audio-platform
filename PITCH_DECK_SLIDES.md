## DESIGN NOTES FOR SLIDE DECK

**Typography:**
- Headlines: Bold, 48-60pt, high contrast
- Body text: 24-28pt (readable from distance)
- Data/stats: 72-96pt (make numbers pop)
- Font: Brutalist sans-serif (Helvetica Neue Bold, Inter, or similar)

**Colour Palette:**
- Background: Dark grey (#1a1a1a) or white (#ffffff)
- Primary text: White or black (high contrast)
- Accent 1: Cyan (#00d4ff) - for positive data, time saved
- Accent 2: Amber (#f6ab00) - for costs, attention points
- Accent 3: Purple (#8b5cf6) - for innovation, tech
- Alert: Red (#ef4444) - for pain points, wasted time

**Visual Style:**
- Brutalist aesthetic: Bold blocks, high contrast, geometric shapes
- Minimal decoration: Let data and words speak
- Screenshots: Real UI, not mockups (build credibility)
- Charts: Simple, bold, easy to scan from distance
- Icons: Minimal, geometric, consistent style

**Slide Layout:**
- Generous white space (or dark space)
- One main idea per slide
- Numbers should be massive and bold
- Bullets: 3-5 max, short phrases, not paragraphs
- Consistent layout throughout
