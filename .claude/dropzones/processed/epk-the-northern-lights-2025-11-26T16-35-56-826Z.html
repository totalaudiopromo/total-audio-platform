Biography

The Northern Lights are an electronic duo from Manchester, UK. Blending ambient soundscapes with driving beats, they've carved out a unique space in the UK electronic scene since 2019. Their debut EP "Aurora" caught the attention of BBC Radio 6 Music, leading to extensive playlist support and a Maida Vale session.
