# sadact

_left-field club melancholy_

## Bio

sadact (Chris Schofield) is a UK-based producer crafting emotionally-charged electronic music that sits somewhere between club functionality and introspective listening. With 5+ years in radio promotion and a deep understanding of the underground music landscape, sadact brings an authentic industry perspective to experimental club sounds.

Supported by BBC Radio 6 Music, Rinse FM, and featured at Boiler Room events, sadact's productions blend melancholic melodies with driving rhythms, creating dancefloor moments that resonate long after the lights come up.
