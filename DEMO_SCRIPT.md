## Demo Tips & Best Practices

### Energy & Pacing

**Do:**
- Speak with enthusiasm (you're excited about saving them time)
- Pause after key moments (let value sink in)
- Make eye contact (don't just stare at screen)
- Use hand gestures (emphasise key points)

**Don't:**
- Rush through demo (they need to absorb)
- Apologise for tool limitations (own it)
- Get lost in technical details (stay high-level)
- Let silence become awkward (pause is powerful, but know when to continue)

---

### Handling Technical Issues

**If Audio Intel doesn't load:**
1. Say: "Let me show you screenshots while this loads" (have backup ready)
2. Switch to prepared screenshots
3. Maintain energy (don't panic)

**If pitch generation takes longer than 30 seconds:**
1. Say: "While the AI works, let me explain what it's doing..." (fill time productively)
2. Explain personalisation logic
3. By time you're done explaining, pitch should be ready

**If Campaign Tracker demo fails:**
1. Say: "Let me show you the client report export first, then circle back" (reorder demo)
2. Use backup video or screenshots
3. Offer to send full demo video later
