## How to Use These Stories

### LinkedIn Posts

- Use as case studies
- Focus on business value and ROI
- Include specific numbers and results

- Break into multiple tweets
- Focus on before/after contrast

## Story Collection Tips

- Ask clients for permission to use their stories
- Anonymize if requested
- Focus on quantifiable results
- Get quotes in their own words
- Update regularly with new successes
