## CONTENT GENERATION APPROACH

### Option A: Manual Writing (Highest Quality)

- **Time**: 3-4 hours per page
- **Quality**: 10/10 truthfulness
- **Scalability**: Low (13 pages/month max)

### Option B: AI-Assisted with Human Review (Recommended)

- **Time**: 1-2 hours per page
- **Quality**: 9/10 truthfulness with validation
- **Scalability**: Medium (30 pages/month)
- **Process**:
  1. Feed research data to Claude
  2. Generate section-by-section
  3. Human review for accuracy
  4. Verify all claims against sources
  5. Edit for voice/tone consistency

### Option C: Hybrid Batch Generation

- **Time**: 30-45 minutes per page
- **Quality**: 8/10 with strict validation
- **Scalability**: High (60 pages/month)
- **Process**:
  1. Batch research for 10 pages at once
  2. Generate all FAQ sections together
  3. Generate all "Understanding [Station]" sections together
  4. Template-based assembly
  5. Batch validation and review
